What is the Background Fetch API?

Allows large or long-running downloads to run in the background. A service worker is used to handle download completion functionality.

Why should you use the Background Fetch API?

Large downloads can be handled in a programmatic and non-interfering way. Downloads are automatically paused when connectivity is lost and resumed when connectivity is regained, simplifying download management.
