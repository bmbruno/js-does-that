What is the IndexedDB API?

IndexedDB provides a transactional database for storing large amounts of structured data on the client.

Why should you use the IndexDB API?

Client-side storage solutions like localStorage and sessionStorage are limited to handling small amounts of simple key/pair data (usually 5MB per origin). IndexDB provides multiple gigabytes of storage, depending on the browser implementation.

Permission Required?
