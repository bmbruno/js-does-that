What is the IndexDB API?

IndexedDB provide a transactional database for storing large amounts of structured data on the client.

Why should you use the IndexDB API?

Web storage solutions like localStorage and sessionStorage are limited to handling small amounts of simple key/pair data (currently 5MB per origin). IndexDB provides multiple gigabytes of storage, depending on the browser implementation.
