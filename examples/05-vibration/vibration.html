What is the Vibration API?

Vibrate a device using timed patterns. You cannot control the strength of the vibration. Do not overuse vibrations to annoy users or use excessive amounts of energy.

Extra-special note: Safari on iOS (and thus all iOS browsers) do not support the Vibration API.

Why should you use the Vibration API?

Provide haptic feedback when the user interacts with certain elements or interactivity in games.
